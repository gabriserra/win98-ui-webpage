Introducing myself

I'm a Ph.D. student at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, situated in Pisa, Italy. Proudly, I work in the Real-Time Systems Laboratory (ReTiS) under the supervision of Prof. Giorgio Buttazzo and Prof. Alessandro Biondi.

I received a Master degree in Embedded Computing Systems, a course offered jointly by Università di Pisa & Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna. I graduated cum laude with a thesis on IPC mechanisms and exception handling for an RT safety-critical kernel, sponsored by Rete Ferroviaria Italiana.

I am a co-founder and CTO at DeliveryAggregator an incorporated company specialized in food delivery.
